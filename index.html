Ctrl-Cloud

Synopsis

Ctrl-Cloud is a multi-platform software developed to unify all your copy and paste needs! From your desktop to your phone, you can seamlessly move snippets of text, images, and videos without any hassle! The simplicity of the mobile application will be how the user has access to a “chat-styled” feature that contains the full functionality to copy, paste and share their findings.

Motivation

We ambitiously desire to design and implement a cross-platform software that integrates a seamless copy-paste feature. We have chosen our platforms based upon what we feel we have the most experience in. We believe our strengths revolve around Java, Ruby, HTML/CSS, and Android which should provide us with the knowledge base to produce a user friendly and fully functioning application.
